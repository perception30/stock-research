Data-driven stock research combining multiple analysis sources:

- **Technical Analysis** — RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, Fibonacci levels, moving averages via Polygon.io
- **Fundamental Analysis** — Revenue growth, earnings surprises, P/E ratios, free cash flow via yfinance
- **Political Signals** — Congressional trading activity, government contracts, lobbying data via Quiver Quantitative
- **Sentiment Analysis** — News sentiment scoring via Finnhub

Each stock is scored on a composite 0-100 scale with a separate risk rating to identify the best risk-adjusted opportunities.
